About me

I am a PhD Student at the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology, part of University Politehnica of Bucharest. My thesis focuses on unsupervised deep learning and clustering algorithms. My PhD advisor is Prof. Bogdan IONESCU, the Coordinator of the Multimedia Lab @ CAMPUS-UPB.

My research interests consist of, but are not limited to:

  • Machine learning
  • Multimedia retrieval
  • Computer vision
  • Generative neural nets
  • Clustering techniques
  • Unsupervised learning algorithms
